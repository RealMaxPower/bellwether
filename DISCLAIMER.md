## Data: facts, attribution, and what is *not* redistributed

- **Numerical economic values are facts**, and facts are not protected by copyright in the
  United States (*Feist Publications, Inc. v. Rural Telephone Service Co.*, 499 U.S. 340
  (1991)). Bellwether reproduces such values with attribution to their primary source.
- **Quoted commentary** (e.g. excerpts from ISM monthly Reports on Business) is included as
  limited, attributed **fair-use** excerpts for commentary and education — not bulk republication.
- **Proprietary, licensed datasets are deliberately not redistributed.** ISM industry-level
  detail that the project has not licensed is shown as the maintainer's clearly-labelled
  **editorial estimates** (each carries an "Estimate" badge in the UI), not as ISM data.
- A full, per-source ledger of provenance and licensing status lives in
  [data/LICENSING.md](data/LICENSING.md). Anyone needing official or bulk datasets should
  obtain them directly from the source (e.g. license from ISM, or pull from FRED under its terms).
